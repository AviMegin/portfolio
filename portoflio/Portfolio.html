Exploring Styles: Going for a Magazine Vibe

When I started designing my website, I aimed for a cool magazine look. Picture flipping through colorful pages online. But, after checking with users, it turned out it wasn't so practical. IF everytime you wanted to see more content, you had to go through a slow page flipping animation, it would certainly become annoying

Trying Something New: Book Vibes with Sideways Scrolling

I thought, what if the site felt like reading a book, scrolling sideways? It seemed interesting, but when people tried it, it got confusing. It didn't work as well as I hoped.

Learning and Revisiting: Back to Parallax Scrolling with a Newspaper Twist

I took a step back and learned from the experiments. Going back to my first idea of parallax scrolling, I added a touch of a traditional newspaper layout. This time, it felt just rightâ€”keeping things interesting but also easy to use.In the end, my website got its groove with a mix of parallax scrolling and a regular newspaper layout. This way, it's easy for people to click around while still looking good. Learning from mistakes and listening to users helped me get there, making the site both cool and user-friendly.
